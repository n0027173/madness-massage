Welcome to Massage Madness

Thank you for your interest in Massage Madness! Massage therapy is a wonderful and valuable tool for the prevention of soft tissue injuries and to guide your body back to a more balanced state. Massage therapy is so much more than a luxurious or indulgent experience. At Capstone Massage Therapy, the most fundamental part of the massage and focus is on you. Your massage experience is tailored to meet your needs. Whether it is a headache, sore muscles, stress, or you just need to get away, we have "got your back."

Benfefits of Massage

  • Alleviates depression
  • Eases arthritis
  • Lessens back pain, especially lower back pain
  • Lower Intensity and frequency of migraines and headaches are lowered
  • Prevention of injuries and to rev up the muscles for better performance (pre / post workouts)
  • Reduces general aches and pains in the muscle and soft tissues
  • Reduces repetitive stress injuries
  • Reduces stress
  • Strengthens the immune system
